Built with security in mind

Every layer of OtpVault is designed to keep your secrets safe — from encryption to infrastructure.

🔒

Zero-Knowledge Encryption

Your vault is encrypted with AES-256-GCM before leaving your device. The encryption key is derived from your password using Argon2id and is never transmitted to any server.

☁️

Encrypted Cloud Backup

Your encrypted vault is automatically backed up to Neon PostgreSQL. Sign in with the same email on any device to restore your codes — the server never sees plaintext data.

📱

Works on Every Device

Desktop native app (Windows) plus mobile PWA that works offline. One encrypted vault, seamlessly accessible across all your devices through cloud sync.

⚡

Blazing Fast & Lightweight

Built with Rust and Tauri on desktop, and React + Vite on mobile. Uses a fraction of the resources of Electron-based apps. Launches instantly.

🔐

Works Offline

Both desktop and mobile versions work fully offline. Your 2FA codes are generated locally on your device — no internet connection required for daily use.

🛡️

Open Source & Auditable

Every line of code is public on GitHub under MIT license. The entire build pipeline is transparent — anyone can verify there are no backdoors.

Two platforms, one vault

Choose the experience that fits your workflow. Your encrypted vault syncs seamlessly between both.

How it Works

Get started in three simple steps — same flow on desktop and mobile.

1

Create Your Encrypted Vault

Sign up with your email and password. Your password never leaves your device — it's used to derive your encryption key locally.

→
2

Add Your Accounts

Scan QR codes, upload a QR image, or enter the secret key manually. Supports SHA1/SHA256/SHA512, 6-8 digits, and custom step intervals.

→
3

Sync Across Devices

Sign in with the same email on desktop or mobile. Your encrypted vault syncs automatically — seamless access everywhere.
